Before You Impress

Four rules for the registrar.

  1. i.
    Proofread before signing.
    Typos cannot be edited — only reissued. A reissue costs gas and leaves a visible scar.
  2. ii.
    Use the student's canonical name.
    The hash is case-sensitive. “Aarav Sharma” and “aarav sharma” produce different fingerprints.
  3. iii.
    Upload the final PDF.
    Once pinned to IPFS, its content is immutable. Any later edit would break the link.
  4. iv.
    Verify the student's wallet first.
    Soulbound means soulbound. A misaddressed certificate cannot be recalled — only reissued to the correct wallet.
Editor's margin On reissue: if you discover an error after signing, toggle Reissue Existing above and enter the old token №. The old certificate is marked revoked and a pointer (replacedBy) is written to the new one. The original remains legible on-chain as a record of the correction; nothing is ever truly erased.

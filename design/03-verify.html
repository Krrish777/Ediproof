II.

A glossary for the uninitiated.

Five terms you will meet on this page. No jargon uncovered is no jargon useful.

Soulbound Token

An ERC-721 that cannot be transferred after mint. Permanent residence; no secondary market. A diploma you cannot sell.

IPFS

The InterPlanetary File System. A decentralised store for the PDF itself, keyed by its own content. Change the file, change the key.

keccak256

The hash function Ethereum uses. It maps any input — short or long — onto 32 bytes. One letter changed, a wholly different fingerprint.

Sepolia

Ethereum's test network. The same machinery as mainnet, but with money of no value — the traditional waterway for first voyages.

Revocation

A certificate, though immutable, may be marked as no longer valid by its original issuer. The record of the revocation is itself permanent.

Reissue

When a typo must be corrected, a new certificate is issued and the old one marked revoked with a pointer to the new. The scar is visible.
